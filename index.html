About Us

Refresh Rapid is a movement inspiring design, development, and progress.
The goal is simple: We set the stage for sharp designers and developers to share refreshing perpectives and expertise; in the hopes of inspiring and contributing to the momentum that is the Rapid City tech community.

We are always open to suggestions for discussion topics, looking for speakers and sponsors and would love your involvment.

Refresh Manifesto

Refresh Rapid is one of many cities and communities that shares in the Refresh Manifesto:

  • Let's Gather Great Minds
  • Let's Share All of Our Knowledge
  • Let's All Grow and Learn
  • Let's Promote Local Talent
  • Let's Make Our Community Better

Our Approach

Our approach to Refresh Rapid is to provide meetups that feature short, thought-provoking and attention span friendly sessions by speakers followed by interesting discussions. The goal is to deliver great content, inspire the audience and ignite the community. In addition, you get a chance to network with other local people that you would like to meet.

Our Vision

We envision Rapid City as a modern tech city, the connected hub of Western South Dakota, melding our rich culture with the future opportunities that modern technology brings us.

Our Objective

To be an educational center and resource for those engaged in the technical arts in Rapid City. With projects such as NodeSchool, we hope to provide those with the desire to learn an encouraging atmosphere to develop and grow.
